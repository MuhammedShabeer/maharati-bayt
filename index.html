The Untapped Opportunity in Qatar

Two massive gaps co-existing in the same city.

Talent Gap

Thousands of educated women on family visas (Husband sponsorship) are unemployed. They have Masters/Bachelors degrees but are restricted by childcare duties (7 AM - 1:30 PM) or lack of flexible options.

"My sister has a Master's in Commerce but sits at home bored because no full-time job fits her schedule."

SME Struggle

Small businesses in Doha cannot afford full-time staff (Salary + Visa + Accommodation + Flights) for every role. They drastically need fractional help for accounting, admin, and social media.
